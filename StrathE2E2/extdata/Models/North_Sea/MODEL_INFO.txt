Model region      : North_Sea
StrathE2E2 version: 4.0.1

Model variant name: 1970-1999
Release date      : 30-05-2022
Implementation originally produced in the NERC Marine Ecosystem Programme (https://marine-ecosystems.org.uk/)
Driving data represent a climatological annual cycle for the period 1970-1999
Hydrodynamnic driving data from NEMO-ERSEM 7km model (https://www.uk-ssb.org/science_components/work_package_4/)
Nutrient driving data from ICES and BODC data archives and informed by NEMO-ERSEM
Atmospheric driving data from EMEP (https://www.emep.int/)
River driving data from a statistical models of rainfall river flow and nutrent monitoring data (https://strathprints.strath.ac.uk/18588/6/strathprints018588.pdf)
Fishing data from ICES and STECF (https://www.ices.dk/data/dataset-collections/Pages/Fish-catch-and-stock-assessment.aspx and https://stecf.jrc.ec.europa.eu/dd/fdi)
Setup parameters modified May 2022 to be consistent with StrathE2E 4.0.0
Documentation: https://www.marineresourcemodelling.maths.strath.ac.uk/resources/StrathE2E2/documents/4.0.0/StrathE2E2_North_Sea_model.pdf
